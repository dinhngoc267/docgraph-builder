Title:Reflections on Pandemic (H1N1) 2009 and the International Response
Authors: ['Leung, Gabriel M.', 'Nicoll, Angus']
Content: Gabriel Leung and Angus Nicoll provide their reflections on the international response to the 2009 H1N1 influenza pandemic, including what went well and what changes need to be made in anticipation of future flu pandemics.
